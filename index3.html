Suzuki Motor Corporation (Japanese:, Hepburn: Suzuki Kabushiki-Gaisha) is a multinational Japanese corporation based in Minami-ku, Hamamatsu. Suzuki makes cars, motorbikes, ATVs, outboard marine engines, wheelchairs, and a range of other small internal combustion engines. Suzuki was the eleventh largest carmaker in the world by output in 2016. Suzuki employs about 45,000 people and operates 35 manufacturing plants in 23 countries, as well as 133 distributors in 192 countries. Automobile sales in the United States are the tenth largest in the world, while domestic sales are the third highest in the country.
